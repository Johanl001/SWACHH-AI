## Welcome to SWACHH-AI 🌿

The SWACHH-AI Citizen App rewards you for disposing waste correctly. Every time you use a SWACHH-AI smart bin, you earn **Green Credits** — which you can redeem for real rewards like bus passes, grocery vouchers, and more.

**Available on:** Android & iOS
**Languages:** English | हिंदी | मराठी

---

## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Download & Install

- **Android:** Search "SWACHH-AI" on Google Play Store → Install
- **iOS:** Search "SWACHH-AI" on App Store → Get

### 1.2 Create Your Account

1. Open the app → Tap **"Get Started"**
2. Enter your **mobile number** → Tap **Send OTP**
3. Enter the 6-digit OTP from SMS
4. Fill in: **Name**, **City** (Delhi / Mumbai / Pune), **Language preference**
5. Tap **Create Account** ✅

> Your account is created! You start as a **Bronze Scavenger** with 0 Green Credits.

### 1.3 Changing Language

Go to **Profile → Settings → Language** → Choose English / हिंदी / मराठी

## 2. How Green Credits Work

Every time you dispose waste at a **SWACHH-AI smart bin**, the AI camera at the bin identifies what you threw in and awards you credits:

| Waste Type | Green Credits | Why |
|-----------|--------------|-----|
| 🌿 Organic | 10 credits | Compostable — great for soil |
| 📄 Paper | 15 credits | Easily recyclable |
| 🧴 Plastic | 25 credits | High environmental impact reduction |
| 🔩 Metal | 30 credits | Highest recyclability value |

**Your Rank Multiplier increases your earnings:**

| Rank | Credits Multiplier |
|------|--------------------|
| 🟤 Bronze Scavenger | 1.0× (base) |
| ⚪ Silver Sorter | 1.2× |
| 🟡 Gold Guardian | 1.5× |
| 🔵 Platinum Pioneer | 1.8× |
| 💎 Diamond Defender | 2.5× |

**Example:** A Diamond Defender throwing Plastic earns 25 × 2.5 = **62 credits!**

## 3. Using a Smart Bin

1. **Walk up** to any SWACHH-AI smart bin (green leaf logo on the lid)
2. **Open the bin** and drop your waste in the correct compartment
3. The AI camera detects your waste **automatically** within 5 seconds
4. You receive a **push notification** on your phone:
   > "✅ You disposed Plastic correctly! +25 Green Credits earned."
5. Credits appear in your **GreenDashboard** instantly

> **Important:** You must be **logged in** and your phone must be **nearby** (Bluetooth or bin QR scan — depending on your bin model) for credits to link to your account.

### Bin Compartments

| Compartment | Colour | For |
|------------|--------|-----|
| 🟢 Green | Dark Green | Organic / Wet waste |
| 🔵 Blue | Blue | Paper / Dry recyclables |
| 🟡 Yellow | Yellow | Plastic |
| ⚫ Black | Black | Metal |

## 7. Live Map

Tap **🗺️ Live Map** to see all SWACHH-AI bins near you in real time:

| Pin Colour | Bin Status | Meaning for You |
|-----------|-----------|----------------|
| 🟢 Green | < 60% full | Go here — plenty of space |
| 🟡 Yellow | 60–80% full | Still usable |
| 🔴 Red | > 80% full | Almost full — go to nearest green bin |

### How to Use the Map

1. Tap **🗺️ Live Map** on the home screen
2. Your location appears as a **blue dot**
3. Nearby bins are shown as colour-coded pins
4. Tap a bin pin to see: distance, fill level, last update
5. Tap **"Navigate"** to get directions via Google Maps

> **Pro tip:** Use the map to beat the red bins! If you arrive at a 🟢 Green bin and dispose your waste before it turns 🔴 Red, you complete the quest "Reach a bin before it's full."

## 12. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: I disposed waste but didn't get credits. What do I do?**
A: Ensure your phone is connected to the internet and you were logged in. Credits can take up to 2 minutes. If still missing, go to Profile → Support → Report Missing Credit.

**Q: Can I use bins in another city?**
A: Yes! Your account works in Delhi, Mumbai, and Pune. Credits from any city all go to the same balance.

**Q: Do credits expire?**
A: Credits are valid for 12 months from the date earned.

**Q: Can I give my credits to someone else?**
A: Not directly. But you can donate credits to a school or environmental cause from the Redemption Store.

**Q: The bin near me shows Red but I need to dispose urgently. What do I do?**
A: Use the Live Map to find the nearest 🟢 Green bin. If all nearby bins are 🔴 Red, report it via the app — it alerts the municipality immediately.
